Your caret deserves better than just blinking. Give it a glowing streak that screams across the editor like Kaneda peeling out of Neo Tokyo, or a lightcycle carving a fresh line across the Grid. Smooth Caret Trail rides the same easing as IntelliJ's native smooth caret, so it looks fast without feeling silly.

What you can do

  • Trail shape: straight, bezier curve, sine wave for when you want some wiggle, or random per dash.
  • Number of lines: one, two, or three. Three is canonically the most cyberpunk.
  • Colors: pick a head and a tail and the plugin builds a gradient. Match them for a flat look.
  • Glow: a halo around the trail, with its own color, for that amazing glowing effect.
  • Thickness: from "did anything just happen" to "is my IDE on fire".

Tuned in Settings → Editor → Smooth Caret Trail

Pairs best with smooth caret movement enabled in Settings → Editor → General → Appearance. The trail piggybacks on the registry keys editor.smooth.caret.duration and editor.smooth.caret.curve.parametric.factor, so anything that tunes the platform caret tunes the trail.
